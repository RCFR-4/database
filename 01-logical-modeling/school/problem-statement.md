# Problem Statement

A school wants a system to organize its academic information.

The school administration wants to keep a record of students, including information such as full name, birth date, phone number, and email.

It also wants to keep a record of teachers, including full name, phone number, email, and area of specialization.

The school offers several subjects, and for each subject it wants to store information such as the subject name, workload, and a short description.

In addition, the school works with class groups. Each class group belongs to a specific subject, has a name or identification code, a shift, and a school term, and must be assigned to one responsible teacher.

Students may enroll in different class groups, and the school wants to record the date on which each enrollment was made.

With this system, the school wants to be able to identify:
- which students are registered
- which teachers work at the school
- which subjects are offered
- which class groups are available in each term
- which teacher is responsible for each class group
- which students are enrolled in each class group
- the date on which each student joined a specific class group
