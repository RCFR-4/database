# Problem Statement

A store wants a system to organize its sales.

The store wants to keep a record of customers, including information such as full name, phone number, and email.

It also wants to keep a record of products, including product name, description, price, and stock quantity.

Customers may place orders in the store. For each order, the system should store the customer who placed the order, the order date, and the order status, such as pending, paid, shipped, or canceled.

An order may contain different products, and the same product may appear in different orders. Because of this, the system must keep a record of the items in each order, including the related order, the related product, the quantity, and the unit price at the time of purchase.

The store also wants to keep a record of payments. Each payment belongs to a specific order and should include information such as payment method, paid amount, and payment date.

With this system, the store wants to be able to identify:
- which customers are registered
- which products are available
- which orders were placed
- which customer placed each order
- which products are included in each order
- the quantity of each product in an order
- the unit price of each product at the time of purchase
- which payments were registered
- the paid amount for each order
- the status of each order
